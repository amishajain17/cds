Story

CDS is proud to have been recognized as the leader in tabletop fashion in diningware, crystal stemware, barware, flatware and decorative accessories for the home. Quality and great design are synonymous with the CDS brand. The world's finest tabletop designs which suitably priced for everyone's lifestyle.

CDS's dedication to high quality and exciting designs has been our trademark. CDS offers one of the largest selections of dinnerware. Even though we have a vast assortment of patterns, shapes and materials from which to choose, all materials are strictly monitored for quality assurance.

Profile

When it comes to buying diningware, there are a wide range of options available to you, whether you want a formal dinner set or a casual set for everyday use. Buying a dining set is an important investment. Get it right and you will enjoy serving dinner for years to come. Buy a cheap one and the cracks, chips and stains will annoy you.
